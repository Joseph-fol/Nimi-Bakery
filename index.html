More Than Just Flour

Nimi Bakery is a premium Nigerian bakery brand built on a foundation of love, legacy, and architectural precision. Founded by Dr. (Arc) Amao Funmilayo Lanrewaju, a visionary entrepreneur and architect, the company was born from a deep commitment to excellence in loving memory of Amao Toluwanimi.

We believe that bread is the ultimate staple of the Nigerian home. Therefore, we apply the same structural integrity and attention to detail found in architecture to every loaf we bake.
